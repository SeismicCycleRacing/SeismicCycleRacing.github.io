About

Seismic Cycle Racing is a fun outlet for some misguided bike racing dreams. The name is a combination of two things that have taken over large parts of Calum's life: earthquakes and bikes. Seismic cycles refer to the cyclical nature of strain build-up and release on faults. If you want to know more about seismic cycles, consider taking some Earth Science at Victoria University of Wellington, NZ where Calum teaches.
